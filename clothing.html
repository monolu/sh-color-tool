Reference
  • %c - selected color name
  • %m - item name with article stripped
  • %n - wearer name
  • %s - subject: she / he / they
  • %p - possessive: her / his / their
  • %t - possessive noun: hers / his / theirs
  • %o - oblique: her / him / them
  • %r - reflexive: herself / himself / themself
  • <verb> - conjugated verb, write in I-form, e.g. <wear> -> "wears"
  • @ - "at <character>" when directed at another character
